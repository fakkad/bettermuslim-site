Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Better Muslim ("the App") is built by Nurel Solutions LLC. Your privacy is fundamental to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what stays on your device.

Data That Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Daily prayer logs and fasting records
  • Quran reading progress
  • Personal duas and prayer intentions
  • Charity and zakat records
  • Health and fitness preferences
  • Missed fast tracking

If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

Data Stored in Our Systems

When you create an account, we store the following in our secure database (hosted on Supabase):

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (if provided)
  • Location coordinates (city-level, for prayer time calculation)
  • Preferences: work schedule, commitment level, Quran goal, mosque selection, notification settings

This data is used solely to generate your personalized Ramadan schedule and is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for profiling.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following external services:

  • Supabase — Authentication and profile storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Aladhan API — Prayer time calculation based on your location. No personal data is sent beyond coordinates.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — Mosque search. Location queries are sent to find nearby mosques. OSM Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In (optional) — If you choose to sign in with Google, authentication is handled by Google's OAuth service. We receive only your email and display name. Google Privacy Policy

No Tracking

Better Muslim does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or behavioral data. We do not fingerprint your device.

Notifications

Push notifications are scheduled locally on your device for prayer times, suhoor, iftar, and other reminders. No notification data is sent to external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings within the app. Local data is removed when you uninstall the app. To request manual deletion, email us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
